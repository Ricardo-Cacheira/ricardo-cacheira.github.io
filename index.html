Projects

Temple of Giants was a project developed during my last semester which I took abroad at BUas. It is the first project in which I worked with a bigger team (16 people). I was the programming lead for this project and worked alongside VAs and designers to make our final boss Medusa a memorable experience. This project taught me a lot about working in a larger multi-diciplinary team and the importance of team leads to facilitate communication and project progress.
